MBSG Around the World

Successful trips have spurred us forward to continue further. This year we have trips in the summer, in which boys and girls who are active in our programs and are willing to travel may get the chance to do so. There will be separate arrangements and interviews when the time comes.

Scout Activities

In addition to its annual summer trips and scout camping, Misr Baladna has regular scout meetings for each age group and organizes trips inside Egypt to allow them to discover their country and other scout branches inside and outside Egypt.
